EXPRESSING MADE FUN


The department of english organised a workshop on Theatrical skills as a part of their curriculum. The 2 day workshop was conducted by Pallavi Banothu, who has done her M.A. in Theatrics from Hyderabad. The workshop was conducted from 1:40 pm to 4:00 pm on 21st January 2019 and will also be conducted on 23rd January 2019. Ms. Pallavi Banothu specialises in direction and design and has been conducting workshops since 2011 for school kids, college students as well as the corporate field. She has more experience working with kids compared to adults. "When it comes to children i feel they are more expressive, they want to explore more. Once they overcome the fear, they feel very comfortable. It takes longer with adults because of preconceived notions and the shaming that happens." she said.
