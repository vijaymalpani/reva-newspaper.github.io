Crisis Management. A Brief Report
About the Event School of Commerce Club Activity Report on ‘Crisis Management” 08.03.2019.

As part of weekly commerce club activity ‘Re-inovke’, had conducted an intra class competition on ‘Crisis Management’, Student volunteers from 2nd semester Honors along with faculty coordinators organized the competition.

Ten student teams with two in a group actively took part in the competition. The competition started from 1:00 pm and it was judged by Prof. Nagendra, School of Management. The competition was of three levels, in level 1, A simple case was displayed on the screen and time limit was set for the students to analyse the case. Winners of level 1 were qualified for level 2, which was again a moderate level case that required more logical analysis.
Qualifiers of level-2 finally participated in level-3 were each team had to solve a critical case and logically present them. Question and answer sessions was followed after each presentation. Judgement at each levels were given based on Presentation, Analytical capacity and their level of answering to the questions. Finally one team were declared winners
Over all, the competition gave an insight to the students on how to solve cases at different levels and improve their problem solving ability.
