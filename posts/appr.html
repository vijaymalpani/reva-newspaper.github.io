Appreciations Matter


Appreciate people while they are alive- while they are breathing,smiling,sobbing,breaking and blossoming entities.
I have always disliked people at funerals.
Mainly because of the things they say – She was a great woman, selfless and loving…Why did he have to leave so soon? The world needed more of his goodness.
But did you tell him how good he was when he really needed to hear it? Did you tell him how his warmth flowed out and radiated to people around him? He gave, you took. He embraced, you stood.
People bloom when appreciated. Appreciation may just be words to you but to the person it is directed at, it becomes a reason- to be happy in their skin.
A compliment on someone’s smile can make them glow all day. An employee can probably make it through another assignment without giving up. A mother’s heart could smile with happiness on knowing that she is appreciated.
So do it today. Appreciate the small things that people do like getting a glass of water for you. Appreciate acts of kindness. Notice the growth in people and appreciate the same. Ofcourse, some services are paid for but when you reward someone with your brightest smile and two words of joy, humanity comes alive.
-Shanzha Ahmad
