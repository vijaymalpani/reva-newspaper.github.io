ESPRIMERE-Open MI


ESPRIMERE-Open MIC ,Organised by LITWITS- RUSOA Literary club A Brief Report
About the Event
ESPRIMERE-Open MI
Organized By
Organised by LITWITS- RUSOA Literary club
Date of Workshop
28th February 2019
Number of participants
150
LITWITS-literary club of School of Architecture, REVA University organised: ESPRIMERE- OPEN MIC on February 28, 2019 at the Kalpana Chawla Seminar hall from 9:30-10:30 AM. Students were invited to come up on the dais and share their thoughts, writings, poetry etc on any topic. Several students took this opportunity to share their thoughts and talent in writing and elocution with the audience. Anvitha from IVth semester set the tone for the event with a soulful rendition of poetry. A variety of literary skills were on display as students spoke on various topics such as “Role of Women in the society”, “Beat Boxing”, “Importance of Money” etc along with fun elements such as stand up comedy and Riddles which had the audience in splits of laughter.

The Event was a welcome change for the students as well as faculty members where we got to see a new facet of several students. It was evident that school of Architecture is brimming with creative talent and such platforms are necessary to enhance and showcase the talents of our students. The event concluded with a heart rending Poetry by Nikitha from IVth Semester highlighting the plight of our soldiers who sacrifice their lives for the sake of the country. We would like to thank the team and management of REVA University for their support and encouragement towards organising such student events.
