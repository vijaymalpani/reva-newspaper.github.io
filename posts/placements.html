D-DAY FOR SCHOOL OF COMPUTING AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY


REVA University heartily congratulates Smitha N.S, Venu S.N, B. Kavya, Vinodh .G and Santhosh I. on being placed in VMware. Another jewel is added to the crown of placement and training team as the 5 students from the school of Computing and Information Technology (C and IT) department have been placed in fortune 39th company “VMware”. VMware, Inc. is an information technology (IT) company which is a subsidiary of Dell technologies that provides cloud computing and platform virtualization software and services. It was one of the first commercially successful companies to virtualize the x86 architecture. They came to REVA University for campus recruitment and after lengthy sessions of placement talks and further recruitment process like group discussions and personal interviews, they filtered the students accordingly and selected only a few of them.
It is a matter of pride that amongst hundreds who took part in the recruitment process, our students have succeeded with flying colours and have been placed in such a renowned company with an annual package of 11L P.A.


-MEGHASHREE K.P.
