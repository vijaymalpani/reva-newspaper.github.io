BIG SCHOLARLY DATA

Ever wondered if your research work has been done or not? Ever wondered if the particular topic has been explored? To overcome the mundane task of searching 1000's of research papers and to avoid confusion, here is a smart solution to the problem.
Big Scholarly Data uses concepts like machine learning and bigdata to solve your problem. It is just one click away from the solution .The idea is to generate a smart algorithm which takes data from all the scholars . Hence the name Scholarly Data.
This approach could revolutionise research and reduce the time wasted on plagiarism and help one focus on their research . Writing an algorithm is a challenging task. However, with technologies like python and anaconda(for big data) help overcome this challenge- says Raghavendra Nayaka(Assistant proffessor REVA University , Bangalore).
