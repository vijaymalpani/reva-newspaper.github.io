FOUNDERS DAY CELEBRATION


The Founder Chancellor’s 69th birthday was celebrated with great pomp and glory at the REVA University on January 6 2019.

REVA presented the REVA Lifetime Achievement Award 2018 to some of the greatest laurels of the country. Padma Bhushan Dr. Manju Sharma, scientist and reformist, former secretary to the Government of India, Department of Biotechnology, Padma Shri Dr. Kasinadhuni Viswanath, veteran actor, director and screenwriter and Naada Brahma, Dr. Hamsalekha, renowned Indian composer and lyricist were some of the legendary awardees.

The evening was graced by amazing performances by the School of performing Arts -- a modern version of Dr. Hamsalekha’s songs and a choreographed montage of Dr. K. Viswanath’s movie songs.

The campus was decorated beautifully with a plethora of lights and flowers and was attended by enthusiastic students and faculty members.
