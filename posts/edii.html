A TALK ON ENTREPRENEURSHIP BY SPECIAL LECTURE BY EDII FACULTY BY- CS.DR.R.V.TYAGARAJAN


On 30th and 31th of January 2019,​the students from the department of Management Studies had a guest lecture by CS. Dr. R. V. Tyagarajan, who is practicing as a company secretary and also a visiting professor, has persuaded his M.Com, MBA, ML, M. Phil,FCS,FIII, Ph. D. He is one of the life members of AIMS, along with this he also helps research scholars in completing their Ph.D thesis.
The topic of the session was about how to become an Entrepreneur. The talk was held in seminar hall. He firstly, spoke about what is called self and why should one be aware about themselves and briefed about personality traits and competencies. He kept the class intacted by sharing a lot of interesting and motivational stories about the entrepreneurs which helped us understand the main topic of the session more easily Some of the topics taught by CS. Dr. R. V. Tyagarajan were​ ​Under personality traits and competencies, Entrepreneurial traits and competencies. He spoke about the qualifications and required personality to become an entrepreneur and how one can achieve it.
Then, talking about entrepreneurial traits he covered topics related to Calculated risk taking, Ambiguity tolerance, Locus of control and Types of Locus of control. Lastly, he spoke about the entrepreneurial competencies and how how the young students should start working on it. He spoke about relating topic like how to take initiative, Seeing and acting on opportunities, Persistence, Information seeking, Concern for high quality of work, Commitment to work contract, Efficiency orientation, Systematic planning, Problem solving, Self-confident, being assertive, persuasion and use of influencing strategies.
