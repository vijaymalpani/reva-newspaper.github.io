INDUSTRIAL VISIT TO NSRCEL - IIM BANGALORE


The School Of Management Studies organised an industrial visit to NSRCEL, Indian Institute of  Management,Bangalore on 11th January, 2019. The visit included 24 students from B.BA  Entrepreneurship, a course offered in collaboration with EDII, Ahmedabad. The theme of the visit  "Profiling of Start-Up Entrepreneurs" focused on development of future entrepreneurs.  Students were encouraged by Dr. S. K. Prasad and Dr. Nitu Ghosh to network and interacted with  seven start-up entrepreneurs in teams of three. Ms. Suhruta Kulkarni and Mr. Siddhart Bannerjee of  Gocrackit Pvt. Ltd., Ms. Preeti Rai of Being you, Mr. Gaurav of Settirs Mr. Govind Moghekar of Tasty  Dish, Mr. Bharath of The verb studio, Mr. Bharat of Planform, Ms. Nanda Chougule of Digisec Pvt.Ltd.  were some of the entrepreneurs interviewed which took up to 30 minutes. Students used  questionnaires to profile the entrepreneurs and understand their experiences, achievements, failures,  future plans, etc. The frank manner of the entrepreneurs, who also shared their responses about  their life, entrepreneurial process, fears, inspirations, future plans, financial plans helped the  students get a realistic idea of the field. They appreciated our students and even offered them  internship opportunities in their start-up companies. The interviews and responses collected by the  students would be analysed and stored in a repository and we would be used for research purposes. 

NSR CEL, is an Incubation centre for budding entrepreneurs. Situated in the serene campus of IIMB,  it offers a creative and supportive work environment, that facilitates budding entrepreneurs and  students with entrepreneurial zeal to build their own venture support from mentors and venture  capitalists. The centre has helped around 46,000 plus entrepreneurs till date, mentored more than  4517 ventures, created more than 5400 jobs, incubated 225 ventures. It has a vibrant ecosystem of  industry experts, mentors, researchers and academicians and helps aspiring entrepreneurs to scale  up,find the right direction and propel their business prospects.
