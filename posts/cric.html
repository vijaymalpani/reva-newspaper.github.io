REVA CRICKET TEAM PUTS UP A GOOD FIGHT

The cricket team of REVA University took part in The AIU(All India University) tournament on 16th January, 2019, a South Zone tournament where universities from Andhra pradesh and Tamil Nadu participate. The Reva cricket team defeated other teams in the AIU but struggled in the last 2 tournaments against Kadapa University from Andhra pradesh.
The REVA team of 2018-19 had few new players in the squad captained by Abul Hasan Khalid. The selection camp for the REVA squad began from Dec 22​nd​ to Jan 9th. After testing the player’s abilities,the team was selected to travel to Shivamogga on the morning of Jan 14​th​ by train at 9:00 am.
All the other teams travelled together in a 5 hour journey and were taken to the ​Jawaharlal Nehru National College of Engineering, the venue for the tournament.
The train was said to have all the teams travelling together.The team arrived 5 hours later to be greeted by the JNNC buses where the teams would be taken to the JNNCE university which was the venue for the tournament.
The match kicked off with the REVA team choosing to bowl first. The Kadapa University team started off slow but ended up posting 191 as the target. despite the lengthy practice sessions and confidence, REVA was defeated by 30 runs.


- AG Anshuman
