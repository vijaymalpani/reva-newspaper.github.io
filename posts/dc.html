School of Electronics and Communication Engineering Organises Technical Talk Report on “Power Electronics- Introduction to various DC-DC Converter Topologies, Analysis, EMI/EMC”


The School of ECE organized technical talk on “Power Electronics- Introduction to various DC-DC Converter Topologies, Analysis, EMI/EMC" on 17-01-2019 in CNR Rao Seminar Hall for the third year B. Tech ECE and EEE students.

The resource person of the Technical Talk was Mr. Bhoopendra Kumar Singh, Senior Manager at Centum Electronics Limited, Bangalore, working in the areas of hybrid micro circuits, power electronics and modules-subsystems for more than fifteen years. He has a bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering from Dayalbagh Educational Institute, Agra, U.P. and a Master’s degree in Computer application in Power systems & Drives from IISc. His interests include the design, development of DC-DC converters and planar magnetics for industrial, Defense, Aerospace and Space applications.

Around 150 students from the School of ECE and EEE attended the Technical Talk.
The Technical Talk focused on following key points:
1. Introduction to Power Electronics
2. Switch Mode Power Supply Topology
3. Analysis
4. Hybrid Micro Circuit (HMC)
5. Realization of Space Grade DC-DC CONVERTER Analysis
6. Design Capabilities: DC-DC Converters & HWPS
7. DC-DC Converters developed at Centum
