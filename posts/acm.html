Code Wars V1.0


REVA University ACM Student Chapter A Brief Report
About the Event
An inter school Coding Competition - "Code Wars V1.0" was organised by REVA University ACM Student Chapter in Swami Vivekananda Block.
Organized By
School of Computing & IT
Date of Workshop
26th February, 2019
‚¶Å The Contest was divided into three rounds-
1. Beat the Clock - Participants were expected to code the solution to 15 questions in an hour. Students were judged based on efficiency, correctness and exception handling capabilities of their solutions. Top 10 teams were selected for the next round.
2. Capture the Point- The winners from the first round, faces a series of rapid questions and each correct answer fetches them a point. Top 5 teams moved to the final round
3. Decipher the Code- The particpants were given a decrypted text to decipher the question. After deciphering the question they were expected to solve it in code. The top two teams were the final winners.

This competition saw an overwhelming participation from the students across different schools. The entire process from planning the event, chalking out the logistics, setting questions, evaluating the solutions, arranging IT facilities was done meticulouslyl by our students from ACM Chapter and the School of CIT.
Around 104 students from the Schools of CIT, CSA, ECE, EEE, Mechanical Engineering formed 30 teams and participated in the Coding Contest. The winning teams bagged handsome cash prizes and certificates.
Director Sir, Dr. Sunilkumar S. Manvi gave his relentless support and guidance, to seniors and colleagues in the schoo along with lab instructors, Mr. Palnivel and Mrs. Suneetha G S who gave technical support.
