REVA IS PROUD TO ANNOUNCE THAT MR. MYLARA REDDY C. HAS COMPLETED HIS RESEARCH PAPER


REVA is proud to announce that Mr. Mylara Reddy C. has completed his research paper specialising in “Fault Tolerance in Cloud Computing”.
According to Mr. Mylara, fault tolerant computing is one of the most important domain of computing resources. Cloud computing is the technology where many business and non-business organizations adopting to host their IT services. Failure of software systems would result in additional consumption of computing resources. Also, quick and widespread adaptability of cloud computing technology and also to make efficient use of resources. “In my research, I proposed partial replication of software systems to minimize the cost of fault tolerant system.” he said.
Mr. Mylara Reddy C. has received his B. E. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from U. B. D. T. College of Engineering, Kuvempu University, M. Tech. degree in Computer Network Engineering from Department of P. G. Studies (NIE Mysore), VTU, and is pursuing Ph. D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering, VTU. He has 16 years of experience as an academician teaching courses of Computer Science and Engineering discipline and has published nine papers in national and international conferences and two international journals. He is also a life time member of Indian Society for Technical Education, life time member of Computer Society of India, Member of Association for Computing Machinery.
