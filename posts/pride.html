REVA UNIVERSITY IS PROUD AND HAPPY TO CONGRATULATE DR. SHANTALA DEVI PATIL FOR GETTING HER PhD


REVA University is proud and happy to congratulate Dr. Shantala Devi patil for getting her PhD for the thesis titled “ Design and Development of key management protocol in hetrogeneous wireless sensor Network” from Visweshwaraiah technology University, Belagavi, India. Dr. Shantala Devi patil B.E, M.E, PhD, having 8 years of experience in teaching has been appreciated for her various works. She has published around 10 papers in national and international conference, 11 papers in national and international journal for good repute. She has secured first rank to VTU for M.tech in computer network and engineering. She has received best paper publication award from IEEE in 2016. She has reviewed many journals and international conferences.
“ Wireless sensor Network are the only network which are attached to real world. They are used to sense physiological parameters. As they are attached to real world, it is important to secure them. I started my survey with symmetric E Crypotography and found that it is highly inefficient compared to Punlic E Crypotography. Therefore I came up with an idea of developing Key management protocol using Public E Crypotography for wireless sensor network with the help of chaos crypotography.” Said by Dr.Shantala Devi patil.
