SESSION ON BUILDING A SUCCESSFUL CAREER IN CYBERSECURITY


REVA Academy for Corporate Excellence (RACE) organized an information seminar for working professionals on Building a Successful Career in Cybersecurity on 9​th February 2019. The seminar was conducted by Mr. Ram Kumar G, who is the cybersecurity leader, author and mentor. As it was designed to be an interactive session to help existing information technology executives to understand the scope of career opportunities that have recently opened up in cybersecurity arena, Mr Ram Kumar, RISO – Philips India and a mentor for RACE programs at REVA University shared his experience and knowledge on how to identify the right opportunities and effectively transit into a successful security professional among the highly sought-after organizations in India, and abroad.
The information seminar ​witnessed a large participation from several mid and senior level executives working for various information technology organizations in Bangalore. The meet-up was culminated with a presentation and a detailed orientation on India’s first simulation integrated PG Diploma/MS/MTech in Cybersecurity program offered by REVA University for working professionals.
