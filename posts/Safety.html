“Technical talk on “Safety measures in operations of lifts, elevators and escalators””



About the Event
Technical talk on “Safety measures in operations of lifts, elevators and escalators”

School:
Mechanical Engineering

Venue:
Kalpana Chawla Seminar hall

Description of Event:
As a part of Academy-Industry collaboration placement co-ordinators from the School of Mechanical Engineering organised technical talk on “Safety measures in operations of lifts, elevators and escalators” for the pre-final year and final year undergraduate students in Kalpana Chawla Seminar hall on 08/03/2019, Mr. Ramachandra B from Fuzitech India Private Limited, addressed the gathering and enlightened the audience about the safety precautions, norms and regulations needs to be followed while using lifts and elevators.

Dr. N Ramesh Dean Placements, Dr Raju BS presided over the event and Placement Co-ordinators Dr. Shivasharanayyaswamy, Prof.Robinson P, Prof.Channubasav, Prof.Anand Babu and Prof Somesh from School of mechanical engineering were also present.
