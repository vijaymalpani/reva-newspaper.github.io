Activities by School of Computer Science and Applications

1.Two days Workshop on “Server Side Scripting and Web Apps Development using Frameworks”
Name:Two daysWorkshop on “Server Side Scripting and Web Apps Development using Frameworks”
Audience:School of CSA –Students of IIIMCA(B section)
Date:7th and 8th January, 2019
Timing:8:30 A.M to 4:30 P.M
Number of audience participated:50
Venue:Admin Block Basement Lab-007
Resource person:Mr.Sreejith R, Developer Team-Lead, RLabZSolutions , Kerala.
Event organizers:Prof.Deepa B G, Prof.Manjusree, Prof. Lokesh C K
A two day workshop has been conducted on Students of III MCA (B section) in the School of Computer Science and Applications by, Mr.Sreejith R, Developer Team-Lead, RLabZSolutions, Kerala on 7thand 8th January, 2019, who gave an introduction to the Web Development concepts using web framework Django. The main objective is to familiarize the concept of programming language ‘Python’, which is an all-purpose language. The emphasis was given on clean code and the reusability of the individual components.


2.Guest Lecture on “Importance of Cyber Security”
Name:Importance of Cyber Security
Audience:BCA
Date:10th Jan 2019
Timing:9.30 to 4.30 P.M
Number of audience participated:200
Venue:A.P.J Abdul Kalam Seminar hall, Science Block
Resource person:Mr. Manoj Kashyap, Technical Training Officer, STAR Company
On 10th January 2019, the School of Computer Science and Applications organized a guest lecture on Importance of Cyber Security, for BCA students Resource person was Mr. Manoj Kashyap, Technical Training Officer, STAR Company. He emphasized on the importance of Cyber Law and its all aspects of transactions and activities concerning to the Internet. He also focused on to protect the assets and facilities of the organization. He also spoke about the Cybersecurity on how to secure internet-connected systems, including hardware, software and data, from cyber attacks especially data centers and other computerized systems.


3.FDP on “Statistics for Data Science”
FDP Name:Statistics for Data Science
Audience:Faculty
Date:12th Jan 2019
Timing:9 am to 12.30 P.M
Number of audience participated:30
Venue:Legal studies Seminar Hall
Resource person:Dr. Vinayaka Murthy
A Faculty Development Program has been conducted on Statistics for Data Science in the School of Computer Science and Applications for faculty members on 12 January 2019. He focused on Statistics plays a crucial role in discoveries of science, make decisions based on data, and make predictions. He discussed about how to use the proper methods to collect the data, employ the correct analyses, and effectively present the results. He also spoke how statistics will be help in drawing conclusion for those who are involved in doing research work.


4.Outreach Program- A visit to Manukula charitable and educational trust
School of Computer Science and Application had visited to Blind School Manukula charitable and educational trust on 12 January 2019 to support and spend some quality time with them. In this regard 7 students from BCA 4th Sem and 2 Faculty members. The students initially tried to understand their interest and then planned to carry out activities for both blind and orphan students. The students conducted various activities like: Talk on Cleanliness, Unity in integrity, Games- touch and answer, Clay modelling and Gifts & Snacks distribution.
