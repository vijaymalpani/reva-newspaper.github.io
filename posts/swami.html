REVERED SWAMI SHIVAKUMAR PASSES AWAY


111-years-old living god of Karnataka swami Shivakumar passed away in Tumakuru on Monday morning following a long illness. The seer,who was suffering from lung infection from past few weeks,was put on life support on January 11, 2019.
