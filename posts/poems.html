STARE ME DOWN

									Crushed lilies
									Staring at the ground
									I was here
									To bloom next to you
									You stared so hard
									My existence ceased to be found.
									Yesterday
									I set fire to crushed disabilities
									My sister grew out her hair
									I shaved it
									Cannonballs of societal sluttiness
									Breathing audacity
									Into each other's mouth
									I dare you
									To crush us now
									Metal, fire, revolting desire
									I dare you
									To stare me down.

ODE TO MIND

									Rocking in a shell
									Back and forth
									Back and forth
									Thumping its way to hell
									Eating you inside out
									But you can't tell

									A free fall from
									Reality to illusion
									From sanity
									To confusion
									With no room for logic to take over
									But enough to feel your anxiety hover

									Rocking in its shell
									Back and forth
									Back and forth
									Growing in its crib
									Making you old
									A cancerous child
									Sucking the marrow out from your bones.

PARTS AND WHOLES

									You wreck in parts
									Never on the whol
									Yesterday was a ditch on a smooth road
									Not a nuclear bomb
									Ready to explode
									You mess up once
									One thing, one time
									Multiple times
									In an ocean is a drop sublime
									You're scarred in parts
									You grow on the whole
									Thorns don't mean
									Wilderness cannot be adored.
