Activities by School of Mechanical Engineering

1.Industrial visit to Rittal India Pvt. Ltd..Doddaballapur, Bengaluru.
School: Mechanical Engineering
Venue: Rittal India Pvt. Ltd.
Date: 15thFebruary 2019
Description of Event:
The industrial visit for the students bridges the gap between theory and practice. Keeping this in view, an industrial visit for the students was organized to Rittal India Pvt. Ltd..Doddaballapur, Bengaluru.. The industrial visit to Rittal India Pvt. Ltd. made the students to understand the various types of Machineries used for Punch pressing, rolling, spot welding etc., in the manufacturing process instrument panel housing. The students are also exposed to the robot technology of pick and place of different componentsTesting, Packing and Dispatch of various sizes of panel housings for various applications. About 60 students of 6th Semester of Mechanical Engineering got rich exposure from this visit to Rittal India Pvt. Ltd.

2.Student Development Programme
ABHYUDAYA 2019
School: Mechanical Engineering
Venue: Class Room no: 117 & 118
Date: 15thFebruary 2019
Description of Event:Abhyudaya 2019 (Paper Presentation in Recent Mechanical Trends)

The School of Mechanical Engineering had organized “Abhyudaya 2019 (Paper Presentation in Recent mechanical Trends)” for students on 15th February 2019 from 1:30 P.M to 4:30P.M. By this program students got wide knowledge of recent trends happening in Mechanical field.
Students participated: 49 students with 18 new topics.
