REVA UNIVERSITY PHD RESEARCH SCHOLARS DOCTORAL COMMITTEE

The Research and Compliance Vertical of the School of CIT organised the Annual Phd Progress Meet from 16th to 18h January, 2019. The meet which took place in the basement seminar hall, Swami Vivekananda Block gives research scholars a chance to present their progress in the field of research. The 3 day event was coordinated by Dr. Sunilkumar Manvi, Dr. Vishwanath, Prof. Nikhil Tengli, Dr. G.K.Shyam, Dr. Shantala, Mrs.Nanda Tanga, and Prof. Nimrita.K. Upto 68 scholars participated and presented their research work to a panel of senior researchers and academicians on various topics like Information Theoretic Modeling of Mapreduce Design Expressions, View Invariant 3D Human Pose Estimation and Skeletal Mapping,Optimized Route planning for Traffic Congestion Using Soft Computing etc. The presentations were reviewed by Dr. Ramachandra, Professor, NMIT, Bangalore,Dr. Bharathi Malakareddy, Professor, BMSIT, Bangalore, Dr. D.G. Savakar, Professor, RaniChinnama University, Belagavi, Dr. Shilpa Chaudary, Associate Professor, MSRIT, Bangalore , senior professors from the School and the respective supervisors who gave their expert opinion and advice over the course of the event
Researchers present their progress annualy, which is then reviewed by experts who give suggestions on improving their work. In the next meet these scholars then explain how they applied these suggestions to their work and how the expertise helped them improve their work.
