“Report on Seminar Research Publications, Grants and IPR”



About the Event
Report on Seminar “Research Publications, Grants and IPR”

School:
Mechanical Engineering

Venue:
Hotel Davanam Sarovar

Description of Event:
A seminar on “Research Publications, Grants and IPR” was arranged by “Technical Institute for Engineers” at Hotel Davanam, Bangalore. Six 2nd Semester M.Tech students had attended the seminar.
The objective of the seminar was to give students a knowledge on writing the technical papers, grant agencies and patent filing.
Dr.Sudhan Majhi , Editor of IEEE transactions on Vehicular Technology gave insight into research publications, structure of a technical paper, about abstract etc. He explained about research vs publications.
Dr.Vinod Agarwal, Director R&D, Dayananda Sagar University explained about various funding agencies and how to write proposals. He also elaborated on field areas and related funding agencies. He explained on how to include a schedule and budget in proposals.
Dr.M.Kantha Babu, Director, Centre for Intellectual Property, gave presentation on importance of IPR laws and protecting our research.
He brought awareness on the process and stages of patenting. He explained about essential parameters to be considered for a patent to be applied.
He told a patentable topic should have end utility and novelty. He further explained different stages of patenting like Provisional (Idea Stage), Non provisional, patent pending and patent granted stages.
He also explained about grading involved in patenting like X, Y and A grades. He informed about duration of various stages of patenting.


Outcome of the Event:
Outcome is that M.Tech. Students gained knowledge on publishing a paper, writing proposal and IPR.
