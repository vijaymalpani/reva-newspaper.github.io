1.School of Architecture Faculty members attend Council of Architecture Teachers Training Program at Pune
Ar. Vidya Srikanth and Ar. Shubhi S from School of Architecture were nominated to attend Council of Architecture Teachers Training program on Advanced Architectural theories from 28th Jan-1st Feb 2019 at COA-TRC, Pune. Report on Data for INSIGHT APP From Jan 10 2019 to 14 Feb 2019
Rural Documentation Tour to Barkur and Brahmavar for IVth semester B.Arch students
The students of 4th Semester B. Arch were taken on a Rural Documentation study to Barkur and Brahmavar on 18-19 Jan, 2019. The purpose of the trip was to gain an understanding of vernacular architecture in the coastal belt of Karnataka with reference to socio cultural context, planning, architectural character, climate, material availability and construction techniques. The tour concluded with all students getting an insight into the visual character of both locations Barkur and Brahmavar, understanding the surroundings and history related to Barkur and noting the unique vernacular techniques of constructions followed in the different house typologies. The knowledge gained will be documented, analysed and applied for design of a Tourist lodge within the same context. Faculty members Ar. Neeraja Jayan, Ar. Sanjeev Koppar, Ar. Murali J, Ar. Vidya Srikanth accompanied the students on the tour.
Case study visit to IIM Bangalore for VIth semester B.Arch students
A visit to Indian Institute of management, Bangalore was organized on 25 Jan 2019 as a part of curriculum for 6th semester students of Architecture which requires students to study different aspects of campus planning. The IIMB campus was designed by Pritzker Prize winner architect B V Doshi. The campus is a destination and a pilgrimage for students of architecture and practicing architects, with the architecture of the academic and administrative blocks becoming a case study. The visit helped students in understanding the various planning aspects to be considered while designing an institutional campus and integration of various built and un-built spaces. Ar Shama Parween, Ar Kiruthika Selvi, Ar Nazish Abid accompanied the students on the case study visit.
Case study visit to SJBIT, Kengeri for VIth semester B.Arch students
A visit to SJBIT, Kengeri, Bangalore was organized as a part of curriculum for 6th semester students of Architecture which requires students to study different aspects of campus planning on a contoured site. The SJBIT campus was selected as it is located on a contoured site with level differences of almost 5-6m.Almost all the buildings are placed at different levels giving a varied look to the campus. The students were asked to draw sections across the main spine road at different points, and analyze the impact of contours in planning of individual block.
Skill Development Program on Digital 3D making tricks organized by School of Architecture
A skill development program was organized for VIth sem students of B. Architecture on 31st Jan-3rd February 2019 on the theme- Digital 3 D making tricks. Three dimensional digital models are the best way to demonstrate architectural design.. However, making digital 3D model is quite a complicated process and time consuming process. The resource Person, Ar. Ujjal Halder demonstrated various techniques and tricks which can help students deliver good quality drawings within a short span of time. SDC_Digital 3D
Skill Development Program on Wood cut print by School of Architecture

Site Visit to Chikmaglur for B.Arch 6th Semester students
A visit to Chikmagalur was organized as a part of curriculum for 6th 6semester students of Architecture on 8th and 10th February 2019.
