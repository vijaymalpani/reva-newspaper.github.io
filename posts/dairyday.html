DAIRY DAY: AN INDUSTRIAL VISIT


Students of REVA University, School of Commerce went on a industrial visit organized as part of the course which was educative and to nurture the students on manufacturing and production process on 11th and 12th February 2019.
According to the schedule students of II Sem, IV Sem and VI B.Com Honors and IV Sem B.Com ‘A’ Section were accompanied by Prof. Shalini, Prof. Janaki S and Prof. Girisha.T. The visit started at 9am and reached at 11:30am, after completing the entry requirements, Students entered the industry after being provided a brief introduction about the company and the precautions that has to be taken before entering to the processing unit.
Students experienced different segments of the industry, Firstly they were acquainted with the Research and development practices followed by the organization. Secondly the students were allowed to preparation room were the materials used for developing the product was stored. Thirdly the process of making the product was been monitored. Later they were taken to the actual processing unit, were the students were allowed to observe the making and packing of various flavors and varieties of products produced and the involvement of employees in the entire process was appreciable. Finally, the freezing section of the product where final product was freezed before dispatching.
Each process was explained by the people who were heading the department and guided the students by explaining each process. The company guide clarified all the doubts by students and left the industry by 1.00 pm, reached University campus by 3.00pm.

-Dhamini D
