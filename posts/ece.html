ACTIVITIES FROM EC DEPARTMENT

1.Four days Industry oriented skill development and certification programs.
School of Electronics and Communication Engineering, REVA University had organized four day Industry oriented skill development and certification programs from 15 Feb 2019 to 18 Feb 2019. These programs were scheduled for 1st year students of school of Electronics and Communication Engineering. To inculcate the skill set which is required for the electronic industry. The course was conducted for 1st semester students. The students were allowed choose one out of five courses namely Fundamental of Linux OS, Fundamentals of Web Programming, Design and Development of Quad-copter, Basics of Lab VIEW Simulation, Fundamentals of MATLAB Simulation.
Around 366 students took active participation in the event. These students were trained by industry experts as per the current need of the industry.



2. A session on “Filling Passport Application through Online”
School of ECE in association with "REVA Rotaract Club", had organised a session on “Filling Passport Application through Online” on 12th February 2019 between 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM in G7 Lab, Swami Vivekananda Block.
The session was given by Mr. YashwanthSimha, Secretary, REVA Rotaract Club and supported by student club members.Around 40 students from different schools attended the session and successfully filled the application.

3. Visit to REVA NEST
School of Electronics and Communication students visited REVA NEST on 14th February. Students were curious and got the knowledge about the facility and the support provided by incubation centre.A brief presentation was made by the REVA NEST team covering below topics:
• Significance of entrepreneurship spirit
• Journey from idea to incubation
• Incubation centre and its offerings
• Startups and their requirements
• Support system created at REVA NEST for incubates and Startups
• Working model of REVA NEST
• Seed funding opportunities for Startups – Govt Schemes & Private funding
• Opportunities for REVA University students to work with Startups and incubate their ideas
• Upcoming events from REVA NEST.
• Attended enquiries / clarifications

4.Community Service Activities-Cancer Awareness Program at Shettigerehalli
School of ECE conducted this program on 4th Febraury 2019 for spreading awareness about cancer throughout the village.Pamphlets were distributed to all the residents of the village creating awareness among them about the free treatment been provided in the Government Hospital in Yelahanka from 4th of February to 8th February.Awareness was created about the different types of cancer.Interacted with people and asked them about their problems.A rally was held throughout the village with slogan “Fighting cancer on every step of the way”

5.Technical Talk Report on LabVIEW and its applications in Core Engineering
School of ECE conducted technical talk on” LabVIEW and its applications in Core Engineering” on 6th February 2019.The Resource person of the Technical Talk was Mr. Sunil Kumar V, CEO, VI Solutions, Bangalore. Around 120 students from the School of ECE attended the Technical Talk.The Technical Talk focused on following key points like What is Core Engineering,Importance of Core Engineering,Introduction to LabVIEW,Advantages of using LabVIEW,Demonstrated the LabVIEW tool with examples.

6.Technical Talk Report on “Optical sensor for Bio-medical application in NANO platform”
School of ECE had organized technical talk on “Optical sensor for Bio-medical application in NANO platform” on 21st January 2019. Around 100 students from the School of ECE attended the Technical Talk.This Technical Talk focused on following key points like Development of low cost sensors in diagnosis of Fatal diseases, bio-chip design with all technical parameters, different techniques for the detection of different microbes present in blood,water analysis.

7.Technical Talk Report on “Manifestation of a VLSI Engineer for Aspiring Collaborators”
School of ECE had organized technical talk on “Manifestation of a VLSI Engineer for Aspiring Collaborators” on 21st January. Mr. Prafulla Galphade, Principal Member Technical Staff at Invecas Technologies was the resource person. Around 60 students from the School of ECE attended the Technical Talk.This Technical Talk focused on following key points like Introduction, VLSI Design Styles,today’s Challenges & Opportunities, career Prospects in VLSI Industry
